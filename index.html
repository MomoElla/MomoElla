Our Story

One day in Sri Lanka, a little stray puppy named Momo unexpectedly came into my life. It felt like a little miracle that changed everything.

Discover our story

One year after that special encounter with Momo, I returned to Sri Lanka to see her again. During that visit, the person who had been caring for Momo, and who is now our manager, shared his dream with me.

He had received a beautiful piece of land from his parents, a hilltop overlooking endless tea fields with breathtaking sunsets. Together, we decided to bring this dream to life and create a place where everyone could feel happy, including our guests, our team, the local community, and the animals around us.

Starting from untouched land, we built everything step by step. We created a road by hand, brought electricity and water to the property, and faced many challenges along the way.

Even though I was far away in Japan, I trusted the people on the ground and the dream we shared. Through patience, trust, and teamwork, Momo Ella slowly began to take shape.

A dream that started with one little puppy became Momo Ella.
