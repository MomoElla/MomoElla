Momo Ella

The true charm of Momo Ella lies in its breathtaking mountain landscapes and peaceful temple views. From spectacular sunrises and golden sunsets to the softly illuminated temple at night, every moment offers something special. On quiet evenings, you may even spot fireflies, creating a truly peaceful and unforgettable experience.

Our Story

It all began with a little puppy named Momo. While exploring the mountains of Ella, we met a small puppy who quickly became part of our story. Momo came to represent everything we loved about Sri Lanka — its warmth, kindness, and sense of belonging. Inspired by her gentle spirit, we dreamed of creating a place where people could return again and again, just like coming home. Together with our local manager, Gajan, and his family, that dream became Momo Ella. More than a place to stay, Momo Ella is a place to slow down, reconnect with nature, and feel at home in the heart of Ella.

What makes it Special

Authentic Sri Lankan Dining

Enjoy authentic, organic Sri Lankan cuisine freshly prepared at Momo Ella. Our dishes are made using natural herbs and simply seasoned with salt and pepper, allowing the fresh ingredients and traditional flavours to shine. We invite you to savour wholesome, nourishing meals in a peaceful setting.

Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner are available to order.

Traditional Saree Experience

Experience the beauty of traditional Sri Lankan culture with our Saree Rental experience. You can choose between Sinhala or Tamil styles of draping, each carrying its own unique elegance and cultural tradition. Dress in a beautiful saree, capture memorable photos, or take a stroll through town and experience Ella from a different perspective. This is a unique and unforgettable way to create lasting memories of your journey.
