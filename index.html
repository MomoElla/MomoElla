Our Story

It all began with a little puppy named Momo. What started as a simple love for a small companion slowly became a dream much larger than we could have imagined. As the years passed, we found ourselves drawn to the peaceful mountains of Ella, a place where nature, simplicity, and beauty coexist. Inspired by Momo's gentle spirit and the sense of comfort she brought into our lives, we created Momo Ella — a place where guests can slow down, reconnect with nature, and feel at home. Today, every sunrise over the mountains and every quiet moment shared here carries a small piece of Momo's story.
